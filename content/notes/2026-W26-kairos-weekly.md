# KAIROS Weekly 2026-W26

Frozen as of 2026-06-28.

## Thesis

This week the AI trade kept climbing away from generic model exposure and into operating systems for scarcity. ARK added Cerebras on 2026-06-23 and 2026-06-24, then added X-Energy on 2026-06-25 and 2026-06-26, while also buying Palantir and Coinbase and funding the shift with more Roku and Alibaba sales. The read is that capital is moving toward assets that can intermediate AI after the model layer: front-end compute, power optionality, enterprise decisioning, and machine-native financial rails.

## Top Kairos Moments

1. Compute And Power Optionality Move Back To The Front
   - Fact: Cathie's Ark combined feed shows ARKK and ARKW buying Cerebras Systems on 2026-06-23 and 2026-06-24, then ARKK adding X-Energy on 2026-06-25 and 2026-06-26. The prior 2026-06-18 FERC large-load order still frames grid upgrades and queue rights as part of the AI stack.
   - View: The edge is not only hyperscaler capex. Capital is still reaching for alternative accelerator supply and for power-side optionality that can clear the next bottleneck.
2. Stablecoin Rails Stay In The AI Stack
   - Fact: Cathie's Ark combined feed shows ARKK, ARKW, and ARKF buying about $9.8M of `COIN` on 2026-06-26. Coinbase said in its Q1 2026 results that Base processed 62% of total global onchain stablecoin transaction volume and more than 90% of onchain agentic stablecoin transaction volume, while Circle launched Agent Stack for AI agents.
   - View: Machine-money rails are no longer a side bet. Public innovation capital is treating settlement, wallets, and programmable payments as live AI infrastructure.
3. Source-Of-Funds Rotation Gets Cleaner
   - Fact: Cathie's Ark combined feed shows ARKK and ARKF selling about $45.5M of `BABA` on 2026-06-26 after earlier June adds, while ARKK and ARKW sold another roughly $17.7M of `ROKU` on 2026-06-25 after the heavy June 23-24 liquidation burst. Over the same window, ARK added `PLTR`, `CBRS`, `COIN`, and `XE`.
   - View: The buys matter, but the exits matter more. ARK is increasingly funding AI-control-point exposure from legacy streaming distribution and China-internet optionality instead of just trimming old chip beta.

## Capital Movement

- 2026-06-22: ARKK, ARKW, and ARKF bought `RBLX` while still selling `ROKU`, signaling that ARK wanted interactive attention exposure but not the old streaming-distribution trade.
- 2026-06-23 to 2026-06-24: ARK bought `CBRS`, `PLTR`, `GOOG`, `TSLA`, `AMZN`, and `CRWV` while continuing to sell `ROKU` and trim `BIDU`.
- 2026-06-25 to 2026-06-26: ARK added `PLTR`, `COIN`, and `XE`, then used `BABA` and more `ROKU` sales as the clearest source of funds.
- The signal is that the portfolio is being rebuilt around AI operating leverage and financial rails, not just raw semiconductor or consumer-beta exposure.

## Point To Map

Last week the KAIROS map moved from AI capex toward access rights to power, households, and data. This week the map moved one step further into operating systems. Cerebras and X-Energy are the hardware-and-power side of the stack. Palantir is the workflow and decision layer. Coinbase and Circle represent the settlement layer for autonomous software. The non-consensus read is that AI value may keep concentrating in whoever controls throughput after inference demand arrives, not only in model providers or generic software baskets.

# Kairos Moment Template

## Title

Short theme name. This changes week by week.

## Kairos Score

0-100.

Score components:

- Signal strength
- Capital confirmation
- Narrative momentum
- Consensus gap
- Transmission depth
- Future verifiability

## At The Time

What the market appears to believe right now.

## Oracle View

The KAIROS read. One sharp paragraph.

## Signal

Observed facts, source changes, capital moves, filings, earnings, or market reactions.

Label each important claim:

- Fact
- Inference
- View

Each fact must include:

- Date
- Source name
- Source type
- URL
- Exact fact extracted from that URL or filing
- Metric fields when applicable, such as ticker, shares, market value, weight, or period

## Mechanism

Why this may be happening.

## Capital Path

`origin -> capital move -> sector -> ticker/asset/behavior`

## Value Chain

Map the theme across:

- Upstream
- Core beneficiaries
- Downstream beneficiaries
- Second-order winners
- Pressured incumbents
- Missing bottlenecks

## Market Consensus

The obvious or dominant interpretation.

## Non-consensus View

What may be underappreciated.

## Watchlist

Tickers, industries, ETFs, sources, metrics, and events to watch.

## Future Receipt

What to check in 1M, 3M, and 6M.

## Falsifier

What would prove this read wrong.
